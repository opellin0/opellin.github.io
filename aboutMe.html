About me, the traveler!

Hello, my name is Olivia, welcome to my blog

From the time I was small enough to realize that my backyard and school/church werent the only things the world had to offer, I have had this curiosity about our planet. Everthing from the different terrains, the thousands of different plants and animals that inhabit those terrains, the history of why and how the world and its people are the way they are.

From this curiosity sparked this want and need to travel and see everything this earth has given us and the spark turned to flames when I was old enough to make that dream reality. This page is dedicated to likeminded individuals who want to know how to accomplish all their travel goals.
This page is a guide to what I have experienced so far, and what I plan on discovering. Everything from location recommendations to how many suitcases can I take on a plane will be covered. I am learning to, and wish to share the knowlage I accumulate. So come with me as I explore every opportunity given to me.
